Privacy Policy

At VWSPC, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. HIPAA Compliance

VWSPC is fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). All patient health information is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and security.

2. Data Collection

We collect information through our digital intake forms (via Microsoft Forms) and during clinical visits to provide the best possible care. This includes name, contact details, health history, and insurance information.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Information is only shared with authorized clinical partners (e.g., specialists, diagnostic labs) as necessary for your treatment.
